About this page.

My motivation for creating this website is to spread mental health awareness. For the UT Class of 2024, we felt isolated than ever as we had our first college year experience stripped away from us because of COVID-19. Many classes were shifted online and social distancing required only groups of 2 or 3 at maximum around campus, not allowing us to meet new people. The friends I have talked to in my graduating class affirmed that their first year was lonely than ever before, making us reflect more on our mental health.

I was curious to see if this experience applied to more people our age, as well as people in other age groups and how it affected them as well. I will be taking information from data sets found on kaggle.com, ranging from surveys taken from college students and the general public, search terms on google about COVID-19, text data to people with mental health issues and more. Explore several data visualizations to see trends in data concerning the mental health epidemic since COVID-19.
